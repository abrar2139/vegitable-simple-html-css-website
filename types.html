Potato: Starchy tuberous crop, Solanum tuberosum, widely cultivated for its edible underground tubers.

Cauliflower: Edible white flower head of Brassica oleracea, versatile vegetable often used in cooking.

Carrot: Orange root vegetable, Daucus carota, rich in beta-carotene and commonly used in various culinaries.

Spinach: Leafy green vegetable, Spinacia oleracea, rich in iron and commonly used in salads and cooked dishes.

Eggplant: Purple-skinned fruit, Solanum melongena, used in cooking for its mild flavor and versatile texture.

Pumpkin: Large orange squash, Cucurbita pepo, often used in cooking, baking, and festive decorations.
